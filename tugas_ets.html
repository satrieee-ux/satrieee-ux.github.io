Behind Satriodesigns

Satriodesigns is the digital persona of Muhammad Satrio Adjie, an Indonesian-based digital artist, 3D artist, and designer specializing in concept art, photo manipulation.

  • Artistic Style: He focuses on creating realistic and cinematic scenes using software such as Blender 3D and Adobe Photoshop. His work often features post-apocalyptic, sci-fi, and fantasy themes.
  • 3D Specialization: Based on his 2023 portfolio, he has heavily invested in improving his skills in 3D modeling, specifically utilizing tools like Blender.
  • Collaborations: He collaborates with other artists on visual storytelling projects,

Apart from his artistic endeavors, he has been involved with FounderHub and the Information Systems Expo (ISE) 2025 at ITS Surabaya.
